🐾 1. Origins & CatBloxian Nature

N0ob (C00l3stN0ob) is a unique entity born in the Roblox universe, the only known member of the CatBloxian race. Combining feline instincts with a blocky structure, it stands completely alone in its existence.

❄️ 2. Sub-Zero Trauma & Rescue

During early childhood, N0ob was trapped alone for 3 days in a freezing, sub-zero winter environment. This created a lifelong trauma of enclosed spaces.

The Savior: Michael Jackson discovered N0ob, rescued it from the deadly cold, and saved its life, building their unbreakable bond.

Old Best Friend: Sonic the Hedgehog was N0ob's closest friend during this early era, though they drifted apart over time.

🎤 3. Adoption & Studio Incidents

Michael legally adopted N0ob, raising it with complete fatherly love. When N0ob was 7, the abusive Joseph Jackson tried to whip it with a belt in the studio. Michael instantly stepped in, slapped Joseph, and threw him out to shield N0ob.

👑 4. The Grand Secret

A massive secret remains hidden: N0ob's biological mother is the Queen of Pop, Madonna. While N0ob is unaware, Madonna proudly watches its musical steps from afar.

👕 Physical Traits & Live Avatar

  • Colors: Bright yellow head and arms, royal blue torso, and charcoal-black legs.
  • Feline Features: Sharp cat ears, long cat tail, and nimble paws.
  • Pronouns: Uses it/its in character lore contexts.

🎤 5. The 10-Year-Old Superstar Era

Currently 10 years old, N0ob is taking massive standalone steps into music history:

  • Jackson 7 Group: Joined forces with the original Jackson 5 plus Jermaine Jackson to form a 7-member supergroup, completing "I Want You Back".
  • Solo Project: Working heavily in the studio on its debut solo masterpiece: "Don't Stop 'Til You Get Enough" (DSTYGE).

💖 6. True Love: Rose

While N0ob has great allies (C00l3stB4c0n, Shrek, Po), the center of its world is Rose.

Rose is N0ob's real-life girlfriend. Sitting right in the studio booth, her presence gives N0ob the ultimate confidence to hit those iconic high notes perfectly.
